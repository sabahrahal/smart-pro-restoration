As the insured party, you have the authority to select and hire a repair contractor. SMART PRO, a fully licensed and bonded Disaster Recovery Specialist™, is at your service. Rest assured, we're committed to restoring your property to its pre-loss state.

SMART PRO will meticulously inventory your personal belongings, undertake restoration or cleaning as needed, and securely store them in a climate-controlled facility until your property is fully repaired. Items beyond repair can be replaced if covered by your insurance policy.

Keep your valuables, such as jewelry, important documents, and firearms, with you for safekeeping.

Our SMART PRO Project Manager will collaborate with you on selecting fixtures and construction materials. We have trusted suppliers ready to assist with choices ranging from flooring and paint colors to lighting and plumbing fixtures, ensuring a seamless process.

Our Project Manager and Project Coordinator will schedule repairs with our skilled crews and approved vendors. Any additional repairs necessitated will be detailed in an estimate submitted to your insurance adjuster for approval. Any extra services requested by you will be completed upon full payment.

Upon completion, you'll receive a Certificate of Satisfaction from SMART PRO, along with our one-year written workmanship warranty and lien release, ensuring your peace of mind.
